Chaser!

  • High Concept: Try to capture the criminal.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope they don't get too bored with my game. The players would learn to love colors!
  • Genre: This game fits into a kids genre.
  • Game play:
  • 1. Wait for everything to load.
  • 2. Use your mouse to make bullets appear.
  • 3. Aim before the timer runs out.
  • The criminals generate from the center of the screen while the policeman doesn't.
  • Prototype goal: Get to the criminal before the time runs out.
  • Features: Cool music and very colorful.
  • Setting/Premise:
    • This game is abstract. Made in a make believe place located in the middle of no where. The policeman and criminal are the only character that exists in this world. There is no backstory and there are no tokens.
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience: This game is targeted for children 5 years old and under.
  • Play time: The game is finished in like 20 - 30 seconds.
  • Strategies: The player should first identify where the criminals are located, then where the man is. Be quick about it and shoot them down. You'll die if you touch them, or at least I want it to happen, that's still a work in progress.
